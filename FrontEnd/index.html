About AutoVerse

AutoVerse is a music player application designed to provide users with an exceptional music listening experience.

Our mission is to bring music lovers together and offer a platform that is both user-friendly and feature-rich.

Our goal

Our goal for this small size project is to create a user-friendly music app for all people whom love to listen to music. This application allows users to easily browse, import, and play their favorite music, while focusing on the simplicity of it condenses it compared to other main stream music apps while still adding on a smooth user experience for people that like the minimalistic look.

Features

  • Import music and playlists
  • Play, pause, and skip next track
  • View and look at your music stats
  • Search for music and playlists
  • User profile management
  • Contact support
  • Responsive design for various devices

Theses are just types of features for this coding prototype that I've been thinking about for the last 3-4 months now but didn't have the time due to studying and other extra curricular activities. But I hope to add more features in the future updates of this application.

Our Team

This project was created by Kyle Angeles, a second-year Computer Programming and Analysis student at Durham College with experience in web and back-end development. I am currently working on this project independently to further develop my technical skills and apply what I have learned through past coursework and projects. And I hope to later expand this prototype into a full fledged application in the future as I continue to learn more concepts that I haven't touched on yet.
