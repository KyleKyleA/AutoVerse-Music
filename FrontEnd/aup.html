Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

AutoVerse ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our music player application ("AutoVerse", "the Service").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Account details (name, email address, username)
  • Uploaded or imported music files
  • Playlists you create or import
  • Contact information when you reach out for support

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use AutoVerse, we may automatically collect:

  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (features used, interactions, pages visited)
  • Log data (IP address, timestamps, errors)

1.3 Local Device Storage

AutoVerse may store certain data locally on your device, such as:

  • Imported music files
  • Playback history
  • User preferences and settings

This data is not uploaded to our servers unless explicitly stated.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the AutoVerse music player
  • Improve performance and user experience
  • Respond to support requests
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse
  • Develop new features and enhancements
